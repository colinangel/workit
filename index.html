Reference
Form & Progression
Squats & Deadlifts
Chest tall — eyes forward, sternum lifted
Hips back — sit into the hinge, load posterior chain
Neutral spine — no rounding, no overextension
Rows
Pull elbows toward ribs — not out wide
Squeeze shoulder blades at peak contraction
Carries
Shoulders relaxed — not shrugged
Rib cage down — don't flare
Walk slowly — stability, not speed
Progression
Increase weight when all reps complete with good form
+5 lb every 2–3 weeks is typical
Controlled movement over speed
If form breaks, stay at current weight
Stop or Reduce Load If
Neck tightens during or after exercise
Shoulders elevate and won't relax
Sharp joint pain at any point
Headaches increase
Smooth breathing throughout — holding breath means back off
